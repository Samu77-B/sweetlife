Our door is wide open

Every day of the week

Whether you're popping in for a proper coffee, sharing a freshly baked waffle with the kids, or treating the whole family to something sweet — Sweet Life is a place for everyone. From little ones to grandparents, there's always a warm welcome waiting. Pull up a chair, let the little ones pick their favourite milkshake flavour, and settle in — there's no rush here. It's the kind of place where memories are made over hot chocolates and laughter, where families come back again and again. We can't wait to see you.
